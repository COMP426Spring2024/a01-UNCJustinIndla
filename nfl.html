NFL Timeline
Date Event
September 11, 2011 Newton plays his NFL debut game against the Arizona Cardinals, setting a record for the most passing yards by a rookie.
December 4, 2011 Newton sets the NFL record for the most rushing touchdowns in a season by a quarterback.
Jauary 1, 2012 Newton becomes the first rookie quarterback to throw for over 4,000 yards.
February 4, 2012 Newton is awarded the AP Offensive Rookie of the Year and the Pepsi NFL Rookie of the Year awards.
January 12, 2014 Newton plays in his first NFL Playoff game.
2015 Season Newton is labelled the season's MVP, leading the Carolina Panthers to a 15-1 season record and a Super Bowl appearance.
March 30, 2018 Newton undergoes a career changing surgery to repair his partially torn rotator cuff on his throwing shoulder.
March 24, 2020 Cam Newton is released by the Carolina Panthers.
July 8, 2020 Newton is signed by the New England Patriots so succeed Tom Brady after Brady's trade to the Tampa Bay Buccaneers.
November 11, 2021 Newton signs a one-year deal to return to the Carolina Panthers.
