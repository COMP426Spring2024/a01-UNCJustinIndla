Pre-NFL Timeline
Date Event
May 11, 1989 Cam Newton was born in Atlanta, GA.
September 7, 2006 Cam Newton commited to the University of Florida from Westlake High School.
Jauary 2009 Cam Newton transfered to Blinn College in Brenham, TX where he led his team to the 2009 NCJAA National Football Championship. Here, Newton was listed as the only 5 star recruit and was ranked the number 1 quarterback from either high school or junior college by Rivals.com.
2010 Cam Newton signed with the Auburn Tigers.
October 23, 2010 Cam Newton played a career defining game against the LSU Tigers where he became the second quarterback in SEC history to rush for 1,000 yards and became the new Auburn Tigers record holder for the most scored touchdowns. This game went down as Cam Newton's "Heisman moment".
December 4, 2010 Cam Newton led the Auburn Tigers to the SEC Championship and was named the SEC Offensive Player of the year and the 2010 AP Player of the Year. Cam Newton became a top 4 finalist for the Heisman Trophy, in which he won it by a landslide.
January 13, 2011 Three days after the Auburn Tigers won their first BCS National Championship title, Cam Newton declared for the 2011 NFL Draft.
